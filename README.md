# 🌾 AgroLink SL

AgroLink SL is a Digital Public Good (DPG) web platform designed to connect farmers directly with buyers in Sierra Leone. The platform helps reduce dependency on middlemen, improves farmers’ income, and supports better food distribution across the country.

## 📌 Project Overview

Agriculture is one of the most important sectors in Sierra Leone, but many farmers struggle to sell their products at fair prices. AgroLink SL provides a digital marketplace where farmers can list products and buyers can connect directly with them.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Connect farmers directly to buyers
- Improve price transparency
- Reduce food waste
- Increase farmers’ profits
- Support digital transformation in agriculture

## ✨ Features

- Farmer registration and login
- Product upload and listing
- Online marketplace
- Product browsing
- Direct buyer-farmer connection
- Responsive design (Desktop, Tablet, Mobile)

## 🛠 Technologies Used
